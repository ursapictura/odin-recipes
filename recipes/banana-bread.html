Banana Bread Recipe

Put those ripe bananas to use in the best banana bread recipe ever. Moist and delicious, it's easy to makeâ€”one bowl, no need for a mixer!

This banana bread has been the most popular recipe on Simply Recipes for over 10 years. Thousands of people make it every day. Why?

Because it really is the best banana bread recipe, period. You can mix everything in one bowl, you can vary the amount of sugar or bananas. And the secret to its great flavor? butter.

The beauty of this banana bread recipe is you don't need a fancy mixer! A mixing bowl, a fork to whisk the eggs, and a sturdy spoon to mix the batter are all you need. The sugar amount is flexible. The original recipe called for a cup of white sugar, but most people, including me, do just fine with 3/4 cup, and many are happy with 1/2 cup.

You can toss in a cup of chopped nuts, raisins, or chocolate chips if you want, or put the batter into muffin tins and make banana nut muffins instead. You can even go a step further and make chocolate banana bread.

Ingredients

  • 4 bananas
  • 2.5 cups of AP flour
  • 1.5 sticks of butter, softened
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg, room temp
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract
  • a pinch of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425
  2. In a large bowl, mash the bananas until they are relatively uniform puree.
  3. Add softened butter, sugar, vanilla extract, and 1 egg to your banana mixture. Whisk or stir to combine and set aside.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine four, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Whisk to combine.
  5. Slowly add flour mixture to your banana mix. Fold to combine.
  6. Pour batter into a greased loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 30 minutes or until loaf is golden brown
  8. Serve with butter or nutella.
