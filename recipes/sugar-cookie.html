Sugar Cookie Recipe

These quick and easy sugar cookies are a delightfully sweet and delicious. They're sure to delight at events and gatherings.

When it comes to baking, it doesn't get any simpler than thw sugar cookie. But simple doesn't always mean easy. Because of the limited number of ingredients, sugar cookies are all about the subtle balance of simple flavors.

Not to worry though! These cookies have been carefully tested and refined to ensure the perfect balance of sweet, salty, and vanilla. These humble flavors are carefully balanced to achieve an astonishingly complex interplay of flavor. If you follow this simple recipe carefully, you'll get to taste the best sugar cookies of your life!

Ingredients

  • 2.75 cups of AP flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp baking powder
  • I cup butter, softened
  • i.5 cups white sugar
  • 1 egg, room temp
  • i tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and baking powder together in a medium bowl. Set aside.
  3. Cream together butter and sugar in a large bowl. Once smooth, beat in egg and vanilla.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredient to wet. Fold together.
  5. Roll rounded teaspoonfuls of dough into balls and place onto a ungreased cookie sheet.
  6. Bake until golden, 8 to 10 minutes. Let stand on the cookie sheet for 2 minutes before moving to wire racks to cool.
